About Me

Hello! My name is Shane Wasserman, and I am a senior at CSUSM, majoring in Computer Science. With a deep passion for software engineering, I thrive on crafting innovative solutions to complex problems. Beyond my academic pursuits, I enjoy exploring the intersection of technology and design through CAD projects and indulging my enthusiasm for cars.

In my free time, I find joy in coding personal projects, staying updated on automotive industry trends, and expanding my skill set in CAD. Looking ahead, I aspire to contribute to cutting-edge software development projects that make a meaningful impact on people's lives.

Feel free to reach out and connect with me!

Early Life

I grew up in Carmel Valley, San Diego, California. I played competitive soccer for SDSC Surf and DMCL Sharks. Being a team captain for a competitive team sport taught me a lot about leadership, accountability, teamwork, and management. All of these traits have correlated into my every day life and help me with all of my decision making. Although I stopped playing competitive soccer, it is still my favorite sport to play for fun and exercise.

Growing up, I have always been involved in the car community. Cars have been my biggest passion and hobby for my entire life. I love seeing cool cars, watching crazy car builds, following motorsports/Formula 1, and driving cars. My passion for cars is what made me start my own auto detailing business and pursue a career in the automotive industry. I hope to one day own my own car company for designing, tuning, and building crazy custom builds.

Future Goals

I am looking for any opportunity after college to further learn and gain real world experience. My dedication to continue learning is life long and I will be a valuable asset to any team I am part of.
